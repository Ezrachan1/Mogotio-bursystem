Who Can Apply?

The bursary fund is available to:

  • Students residing in {{ CONSTITUENCY_NAME|default:"our constituency" }}
  • Students enrolled in recognized educational institutions
  • Students from financially disadvantaged backgrounds
  • Students in primary, secondary, TVET, or university education

Required Documents

You will need to upload:

  • Admission letter or student ID
  • Fee structure from your institution
  • Copy of your national ID or birth certificate
  • Parent/Guardian ID copy
  • Any other supporting documents
