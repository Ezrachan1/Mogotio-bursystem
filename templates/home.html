This digital bursary platform was initiated and launched by the Member of Parliament for Mogotio Constituency, Hon. Reuben Kiborek, to ensure transparent, efficient and accessible bursary disbursement for every deserving student in the constituency.

How It Works

Step 01

Register & Verify

Create your account with your National ID, phone number, and email. Verify your email to unlock the application form.

Step 02

Fill & Upload

Complete the NG-CDF standard bursary form with your personal, institutional, family and financial details. Upload all required supporting documents.

Step 03

Submit & Track

Accept the declarations, submit your application, and track its status in real time — from review through to disbursement.

Who Can Apply?

The NG-CDF bursary fund is available to:

  • Students residing in Mogotio Constituency (Mogotio, Emining, Kisanana wards)
  • Students enrolled in recognized primary schools, secondary schools, TVETs, colleges and universities
  • Students from financially disadvantaged backgrounds
  • Orphans, students with disabilities, and those from single-parent families are given priority consideration

Required Documents

Applicants MUST upload the following:

  1. Student's Transcript / Report Form
  2. Parent's / Guardian's National ID Copy
  3. Student's National ID Copy (mandatory for post-school)
  4. Birth Certificate Copy
  5. Secondary / College / University ID Card
  6. Death Certificate / Burial Permit (mandatory for orphans)
  7. Current Fees Structure (mandatory for all)
  8. Admission Letter (mandatory for colleges & universities)

About This Initiative

The Mogotio Constituency Bursary System is a digital platform initiated and launched by Hon. Reuben Kiborek, the Member of Parliament for Mogotio Constituency, Baringo County. The platform replaces the traditional paper-based bursary application process with a modern, transparent, and efficient online system.

Through this system, students and their families can apply for NG-CDF bursary funds from the comfort of their homes, upload required documents digitally, and track the progress of their applications in real time. The system ensures fairness, accountability, and faster processing of bursary applications across all three wards — Mogotio, Emining, and Kisanana.
